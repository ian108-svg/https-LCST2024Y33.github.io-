Our Story

FryTop began in 2020 in London, ON, with a mission to provide premium cookware at an affordable price. What started as a small retail store has grown into a trusted name in the cookware industry, known for innovation, quality, and customer service. Whether you're a home cook or a professional chef, FryTop has something for everyone.
